PIDILITE ADHESIVES

FEVICOL CPW

Fevicol CPW is water based adhesive with high coverage. It is suitable for liner fixing and window pasting.

Product Features

  • Very high green strength
  • Good heat resistance for hot filling applications

PIDILAM LM 26

Pidilam LM 26 is water based adhesive used for the lamination of BOPP and PVC films.

Product Features

  • Product has moderate viscosity
  • Good gloss and bond
  • High heat resistance
  • Smooth machinability

Application of Product

  • Great for laminating boards
  • Ideal for lamination of variety of films like BOPP and PVC
  • Best suitable for manual sheet fed laminating machines

FEVICOL 2000

Fevicol 2000 is water based adhesive with excellent green bond. It is best suitable for newspaper jacketing.

Product Features

  • Very high green strength
  • Good heat resistance for hot filling applications
  • High bond strength
  • Suitable and compatible for all standard nozzle applicators

Application of Product

  • Best suitable for carton making and flap closing
  • Used in laminated and varnished cartons
  • Ideal for wrapping laminated covers
  • Great liner fixing and window pasting
  • Perfect for tray and carton formation

FEVICOL 282

Fevicol 282 is a water based adhesive. It is used for manual pasting with high open time and for difficult surfaces.

Product Features

  • Ideal rheological properties, suitable for machine (wheel applicator) and manual operations
  • Works better on low surface energy cartons
  • High open time

Application of Product

  • Suitable to make cartons
  • Ideal for lamination and varnished cartons
  • Perfect for manual pasting

FEVICOL HP 002

Fevicol HP 002 is water based high performance adhesive used for the lamination of all type of films.

 

Product Features

  • Can be pumped on wet lamination machines
  • Very high gloss and bond
  • High heat resistance
  • Very smooth machinability

Application of Product

  • Mostly used for board lamination
  • Best suitable for lamination of variety of films such as PET, METPET, BOPP, and PVC
  • Speciality product for PET and METPET surfaces
  • Ideal for manual sheet fed laminating machines

PIDILAM I 24

Pidilam I 24 is water based adhesive used for the lamination of widely used BOPP and PVC films.

Product Features

  • Good gloss and bond
  • High heat resistance
  • Economical

Application of Product

  • Great for laminating boards
  • Ideal for lamination of variety of films like BOPP and PVC
  • Best suitable for manual sheet fed laminating machines

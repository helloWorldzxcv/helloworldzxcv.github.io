About Us Page

Taarugee Marketing Pvt Ltd is highly renowned distributors of wide range of Bopp Lamination Films and Adhesives. Established in 2006 by Mr.Sanjay Gulati, Taarugee Marketing Pvt Ltd has come a long way from its beginnings because of its ethics and principles. 

Mr Sanjay Gulati started the company with the core objective of providing quality service and supply to the Packaging Industry . Looking at the past years we can say he has been successful in achieving the objective. Mr Sanjay Gulati’s experience and knowledge in the printing and packaging industry has been a major investment for the business. We are pleased to inform you that we are in the printing industries for the last 30 Years in the name & Style of M/S Gurbakshish Enterprises Later M/S Taaru Gee Marketing Pvt Ltd. Was launched in the year 2006 and is dealing in packaging films.

“Taarugee marketing “ was named after a Sikh Guru’s name - Bhai Taru Singh ji , who sacrificed his life in the name of protecting Sikh values. He is known and remembered for his belief for Sikh values and ethics

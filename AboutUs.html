Gurbaksish means “The Almighty’s Blessing”. Using this word as inspiration and starting from humble beginings, Shri Agyaram Gulati lay the foundation for what is now known as the Gurbaksish Group.

The Gurbaksish Group was born in 1970 to cater to the local Screen Printing Industry in the ethnic Indian Business style. The founder Shri Agya Ram Gulati’s vision was to to provide quality service to the ever growing printing Industry and today his dream has come true as the company grows from strength to strength & marks its presence in India and overseas.

The Gurbaksish Group today caters to the Electronics, Advertising, Flexible Packaging, Printing & Footwear Industries. Representing the best in the world in India, the Group brings the best brands to the doorstep af all & sundry keeping pace with innovation & modernization of International Standards, through the Group Companies under its banner.

Taking innovation & development as a vision, the Group has opened Technical Traning Center in Bangalore & New Delhi

The Bangalore center specializes in pre-press procedures & imparts training on the usage of innovative speciality products in the sophisticated laboratory equipped with state of the art machinery

The New Delhi Technical Traning center & Laboratory is dedicated exclusively to the Footwear Industry. Analysis edu birdie.com of raw material & product development to solve every footwear industry problems.

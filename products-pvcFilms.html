PVC FILMS

PVC FILMS After lamination  leaves superior lusture and shine. The bonding strength is excellent with paper and boards with a minimum coat of adhesive. It also enhances the strength of the substrate. These films are widely used in lamination of cartons for pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, toiletries, readymade garments and hosiery. Wrappers for books and confectionery, detergent cakes, soaps, posters, corrugated boxes for electronic appliances, gift articles, consumers products and window envelopes.
